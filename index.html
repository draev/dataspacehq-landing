Why it matters

One governed way to do data — across every domain.

Each domain is a complete, governed unit with the same shape. Spin up as many as you need; every one inherits your company’s rules. And each domain serves its data through whatever port its consumers need:

Full lifecycle — inside Snowflake

Ingest → Transform → Serve

Pick a table, tick “Expose as API”, set a route. mDWH provisions the service, wires RBAC and registers the endpoint. You never touch SPCS, Snowpipe, Tasks or grants.
