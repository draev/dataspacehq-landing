What it covers — full lifecycle, inside Snowflake

Every domain: Ingest → Transform → Serve

A domain is a complete, governed unit with the same shape — layers, owner, access policy, output ports. Write a short blueprint; DataSpace provisions and runs the whole pipeline. You never touch SPCS, Snowpipe, Tasks, dbt or grants.

03

Serve

Publish curated data as governed output ports.
